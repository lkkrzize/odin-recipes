Ingredients

For the marinade

  1. 15g/½oz crunchy peanut butter
  2. 1 tbsp runny honey
  3. 1 tbsp rich dark soy sauce (gluten-free if required)
  4. 2 tsp lime juice
  5. 1 tsp sesame oil

For the sauce

  1. 70g/2½oz crunchy peanut butter
  2. 1 garlic clove, crushed
  3. 1 tsp finely grated fresh root ginger
  4. 1½ tsp rich dark soy sauce
  5. 1 tsp runny honey
  6. 1 tsp lime juice
  7. Small pinch chilli flakes
  8. 100ml cold water

For the chicken

  1. 350g/12oz chicken mini fillets
  2. Sunflower oil, for greasing
  3. Lime wedges, for squeezing

Method

  1. Whisk all marinade ingredients in a bowl. Add chicken, stir well, and marinate in the fridge for 30 minutes.
  2. Soak 8 short bamboo skewers in cold water. Meanwhile, place all sauce ingredients in a small pan with 100ml cold water. Simmer, stirring constantly, until smooth and thickened. Remove from heat.
  3. Preheat air fryer to 200°C for 3 minutes. Thread chicken onto skewers (not too tightly). Brush crisper plate with sunflower oil and arrange skewers with space between.
  4. Cook at 200°C for 3 minutes. Turn, brush with reserved marinade, and cook for another 3 minutes or until browned and cooked through.
  5. Serve with rice, spring onions, and lime wedges. Add sliced red chilli if desired.
